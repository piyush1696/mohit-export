    About Us

    Established in 2000, Prakash Overseas was founded with a vision to become a leading global player in agro commodities. Our focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has earned us the prestigious status of an Export House. We take pride in being one of India's top exporters of Chickpeas and other pulses, contributing significantly to the nation's foreign exchange reserves.

    With operations spanning the USA, UK, Egypt, the Middle East, Far East, and North Africa, we are expanding into new markets in South Africa, Australia, New Zealand, South America, and Europe. Guided by an experienced management team and driven by a passion for excellence, we continue to set benchmarks in the agro-export industry.
